About Us

Discover who we are, our mission, and why Dodawatta Saw Mills is the trusted name in timber.

Our History

A Legacy of Quality & Trust

Dodawatta Saw Mills (Pvt) Ltd is a leading timber processing and supply company based in Kolonnawa, Sri Lanka. We specialize in high-quality sawn timber and wood products for construction, furniture manufacturing, and general building applications.

With years of industry experience, we are committed to delivering durable timber, competitive pricing, and dependable service to contractors, builders, carpenters, and hardware dealers.

Our Mission

"To provide high-quality timber products while maintaining integrity, efficiency, and long-term partnerships with our customers."
