About The Company

Premium Timber Output for Every Scale

Dodawatta Saw Mills (Pvt) Ltd is a leading timber processing and supply company based in Kolonnawa, Sri Lanka. We specialize in high-quality sawn timber and wood products for construction, furniture manufacturing, and general building applications.
